# HypeFiles!
</center>

1. main.hype - The Main Page for your Project. (AKA: The index.html)

2. looks.hyl - The Looks for the main.hype page. (Based off of CSS.)

3. hypescript.hys - The HypeScript for main.hype. (Based a little on C++)
<hr>

## "What do these mean?"  
1. .hype - The Main Hype Lanugage.
2. .hyl - The HypeLooks Lanugage.
3. .hys - The HypeScript Lanugage.

3. projectdetails.json - The .json file that explains Hype everything.
